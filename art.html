THE GOLDEN RECORD

2018
A replica of the Voyager Probe's famous "Golden Record" which contains music and imagery from Planet Earth. The symbols on the are for an alien civilization and both describe how to play the record and provide instructions for finding our world. Reproduced with laser cut plywood and spray paint.

02

DARE MIGHTY THINGS

2022
A 40" diameter acrylic/vinyl graphic inspired by the Perseverance Rover's parachute, deployed on its way through the Martian atmosphere. The irregular color pattern belies the message hidden inside, encoded in binary. Around the rim, the coordinates of NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory; in the center, their motto: "Dare Mighty Things." I laser cut the gray acrylic in two halves with lightly engraved markings for the 320 vinyl segments which were cut on a Cricut and hand placed.
