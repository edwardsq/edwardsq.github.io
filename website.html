A portfolio site from scratch.
I do a ton of engineering projects on my free time and wanted somewhere to show them off that wasn't as generic as LinkedIn or Portfolium. While most people would have (correctly) chosen to use Wordpress or SquareSpace, I had some specific ideas about what I wanted to make and enough programming experience to convince myself to code a custom, self styled, mobile responsive website from the ground up.

PROJECT PAGES

  • Highlights individual projects, with emphasis on process and results.
  • Incorporates Bootstrap for better mobile responsiveness.
  • Keeps the box/card design language from the homepage.

TOPIC PAGES

  • Used in areas where my work is too numerous to be written about individually.
  • Highlights examples which exemplify quality/ability.
  • Gives a sense of the quantity of work.

PRODUCT PAGES

  • Highlights finished products.
  • Mainly useful for when work is either in the far past or not enlightening.

HOMEPAGE

  • How I "define myself."
  • Mobile responsiveness acheived through DOM breakpoints.
  • Bichromatic portfolio thumbnails keep the theme from the top of the page.
