Need for speed.
Normal RC cars aren't fast enough for me, but thats a problem that can be solved with 35g of black powder.

DESIGN GOALS

01 
FAST
Car go fast.
02 
SIMPLE
Not willing to spend weeks debugging a complex system.
03 
CHEAP
Made entirely from parts left over from other projects.

LOOK FAMILIAR?

Inspired by the "Tumbler" Batmobile from Christopher Nolan's Batman trilogy, I used concept art from the film as visual reference to guide the design.

LOW PART COUNT

Taking full advantage of my new 3D printer's 300x300x400mm print volume, the entire chassis and outer shell are printed in just two pieces. The entire car requires only 10 3D printed pieces, a 20kg servo, a motor, ESC, and receiver.
